Welcome to balloon attack, where balloons attack.

You and your laser tank are humanities last hope against deadly space balloons. Unfortunately, their static has damaged your tank. Can you pop enough to save the world?

Controls

Mouse Click : fires your laser. It can be sustained, but be careful.

Mouse : moves the your laser.

Arrow Keys : rotates you around the center of the platform.

Shift : when held, increases your rotation 5x.
